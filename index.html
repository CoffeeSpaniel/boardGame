The Rules

• Each player must take it in turns to move their Knight around the board and collect a weapon to take to the final battle. When ready a player should initiate combat by attacking the other player. Whoever strikes first gets the first turn in the final battle

• During the fight players can choose to defend (recieve half damage next turn) or attack (damage the enemy and reduce their health)

• Attack power is based on the weapon strength, whether or not the opponet is defending and a random chance element. The first player to vanquish their opponent, by reducing their health to zero is the winner!

• The order of weapons from weakest to strongest is as follows:

"unarmed", "dead fish", "axe", "sword"
